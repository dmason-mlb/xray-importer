overviewOverview

This document contains functional test cases for the SDUI Team Page that should be executed manually on Android and iOS devices, as well as through our mobile automation framework.

test-environment-requirementsTest Environment Requirements

  • iOS devices: iPhone and iPad
  • Android devices: Various screen sizes
  • Multiple language settings (English, Spanish, Japanese)
  • Different network conditions
  • Both logged-in and guest users
  • Free and paid subscription accounts
